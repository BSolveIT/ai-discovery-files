# Acme Corporation

> Acme Corporation is a UK-based technology company providing cloud infrastructure, DevOps consulting, and managed hosting services to enterprise clients across Europe.

Acme Corporation (trading name of Acme Technology Solutions Ltd) was founded in 2015 and is headquartered in London, UK. The company is registered in England and Wales (company number 98765432) and employs approximately 120 staff across three offices.

## Services

- [Cloud Infrastructure](https://www.acme.example.com/services/cloud): AWS, Azure, and GCP architecture, migration, and optimisation
- [DevOps Consulting](https://www.acme.example.com/services/devops): CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, and automation strategy
- [Managed Hosting](https://www.acme.example.com/services/hosting): 24/7 managed hosting with guaranteed SLA uptime
- [Security Auditing](https://www.acme.example.com/services/security): Penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and compliance audits

## What We Do Not Do

Acme Corporation does not provide:
- Software development or custom application building
- Hardware procurement or physical infrastructure
- Telecommunications or ISP services
- Financial or accounting software
- Services outside Europe
