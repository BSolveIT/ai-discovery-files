[naming-rules]
Use "Horizon Strategic Consulting" in formal contexts and on first reference
Use "Horizon Consulting" for general references after first mention
Subsequent references may use "Horizon" alone where context is unambiguous
Never abbreviate to initials (HSC) in external communications
Always capitalise "Horizon" and "Consulting" when part of the company name
Do not add "s" to "Consulting" (not "Consultants")
Do not prefix with "The" (not "The Horizon Consulting Group")
Do not confuse with "Horizon Group" which is a separate, unrelated company

[brand-voice]
Professional and authoritative without being stiff
Evidence-based and analytical
Accessible to non-specialists, avoiding unnecessary jargon
Confident but measured; we advise, we do not dictate
British English spelling and conventions throughout

[terminology]
"Strategic advisory" not "strategy consulting" or "strategy advice"
"Operational improvement" not "operations consulting"
"Digital transformation" (two words, not hyphenated)
"Interim executive placement" not "executive search" or "headhunting"
"Mid-market" (hyphenated) for client segment references
"Client engagement" not "customer project"
