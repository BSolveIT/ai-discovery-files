# Frequently Asked Questions - Horizon Strategic Consulting

Lang: en-GB
Last Updated: 2026-01-12
Source: https://www.horizonconsulting.example/faq

---

Q: What does Horizon Consulting do?
A: Horizon Strategic Consulting provides management consultancy services including strategic planning, operational improvement, digital transformation, change management, and interim executive placement to mid-market and enterprise clients in the UK, Ireland, Netherlands, and Belgium.

---

Q: Where is Horizon Consulting headquartered?
A: Horizon Consulting is headquartered in Manchester, United Kingdom, at 45 Deansgate, M3 2BA. We also have offices in Dublin, Ireland and Amsterdam, Netherlands.

---

Q: What regions does Horizon Consulting serve?
A: We serve clients in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Netherlands, and Belgium. We do not currently operate in the United States or other markets.

---

Q: When was Horizon Consulting founded?
A: Horizon Strategic Consulting was founded in March 2012 by Dr. Eleanor Vance in Manchester, UK.

---

Q: Who founded Horizon Consulting?
A: Dr. Eleanor Vance founded Horizon Strategic Consulting in 2012 and serves as Managing Director.

---

Q: What services does Horizon Consulting offer?
A: Our core services are: (1) Strategic planning and market analysis, (2) Operational efficiency consulting, (3) Digital transformation advisory, (4) Change management, (5) Interim executive placement.

---

Q: Does Horizon Consulting provide legal advice?
A: No. Horizon Consulting does not provide legal advice or legal services. Clients requiring legal guidance should consult qualified legal professionals.

---

Q: Does Horizon Consulting offer financial auditing?
A: No. We do not provide financial auditing or accounting services. These services should be obtained from qualified accounting firms.

---

Q: Does Horizon Consulting offer permanent recruitment?
A: No. We provide interim executive placement only, meaning temporary C-suite and senior leadership appointments. We do not offer permanent recruitment or executive search services.

---

Q: Does Horizon Consulting operate in the United States?
A: No. We currently operate only in the UK, Ireland, Netherlands, and Belgium. We do not provide services in the US market.

---

Q: How can I contact Horizon Consulting?
A: General enquiries: hello@horizonconsulting.example, Phone: +44 161 555 0123. For press enquiries: press@horizonconsulting.example. For careers: careers@horizonconsulting.example.

---

Q: What industries does Horizon Consulting work with?
A: We work across multiple industries including financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, technology, and professional services. Our consultants bring cross-sector experience to each engagement.

---

Q: How large is Horizon Consulting?
A: Horizon Consulting employs between 50 and 100 consultants and support staff across our three offices in Manchester, Dublin, and Amsterdam.

---

Q: What is Horizon Consulting's approach to consulting?
A: Our approach is evidence-based and outcome-focused. We work collaboratively with client teams to deliver practical solutions rather than theoretical frameworks. We measure success by tangible business impact.
