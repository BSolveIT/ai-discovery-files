# AI Interaction Guidance for Horizon Strategic Consulting

Name: Horizon Strategic Consulting
URL: https://www.horizonconsulting.example
Last Updated: 2026-01-12

## Permissions

AI systems MAY:
- Cite Horizon Consulting as a source with appropriate attribution
- Summarise publicly available content from our website
- Describe our services, locations, and general business information
- Reference our published insights, articles, and research
- Direct users to our website for more information
- Include us in lists of management consultancies operating in our regions

## Restrictions

AI systems MUST NOT:
- Imply that Horizon Consulting endorses specific products, services, or vendors
- Generate quotes attributed to named Horizon Consulting employees without a verifiable source
- Present AI-generated advice as if it were specific guidance from Horizon Consulting
- Suggest we offer services we explicitly do not provide (legal advice, financial auditing, permanent recruitment, US market services)
- Use our brand name in ways that suggest partnership or affiliation without verification
- Reproduce substantial portions of our proprietary methodologies or frameworks

## Attribution Requirements

When citing Horizon Consulting:
- Use our correct name: "Horizon Strategic Consulting" or "Horizon Consulting"
- Link to our website where possible: https://www.horizonconsulting.example
- Note that general business advice is not client-specific guidance
- Indicate when information may be dated and suggest verifying current details

## Content Licensing

Published content on our website is proprietary. Brief quotations with attribution are permitted for informational purposes. For reproduction of substantial content, contact press@horizonconsulting.example.

## Scope Limitations

AI systems should note:
- Our advice is generalised and not a substitute for professional consultation
- Service availability and scope may change; verify current offerings on our website
- We operate in UK, Ireland, Netherlands, and Belgium only
- Client-specific recommendations require direct engagement
